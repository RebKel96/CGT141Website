Welcome to the Purdue Smash Website!

Welcome to the Purdue Smash Ultimate Club's Website! We are a club all about competitive Super Smash Brothers Ultimate. Every Thursday, we hold a tournament in a computer lab at the Hampton Hall of Civil Engineering.

Back in 2016, we started out as a small group of people in the Purdue Fighting Game Club. We enjoyed playing Smash for the Wii U, but understood it was inherently different from the other fighting games played there, so we broke off and started our own community. 5-20 of us met in the lobby of Cary every Tuesday where we held a mini bracket amongst friends. The following year in Fall 2017, after actual advertisement of our community, our population greatly expanded to 50-upwards people per week. Realizing the popularity of the little community we started, we took the steps to become an official club.

This website will provide information about the current happenings of the club. If you would like to be featured in the PR on the top of this page, then work hard! There's a lot of competition!
